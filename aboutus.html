about us

Midas Touch Production Company is a full service media, marketing, and public relations collective that aims to provide a wide range of services for your creative needs. Based in Southern California, our skilled team of creative directors have years of experience and knowledge in the world of digital media, marketing, and public relations. We offer pre and post productions services as marketers, brand ambassadors, and visionaries. From brand development to image consulting, we specialize in bringing your vision to life through the creative arts. Let our expertises in fashion, design, photography, videography, model placement, and visual arts take your brand to the next level. Our mission is to only produce the highest quality product and services for our clients in order to fulfill their needs. Join us and experience the Midas Touch in full effect. We look forward to working with you.
